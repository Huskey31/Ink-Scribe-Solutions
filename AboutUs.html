About Us



Ink Scribe is a dynamic consulting company dedicated to supporting SETA-aligned organizations,
training providers, and skills development professionals across South Africa. Founded with a passion for education,
compliance, and empowerment, Ink Scribe helps clients navigate the often-complex world of SETA accreditation,
WSP/ATR submissions, Quality Management Systems (QMS), and training documentation with confidence and clarity.
With years of hands-on experience in the education and training sector, our team understands the real challenges faced by Skills Development Facilitators (SDFs),
TVET colleges, NGOs, and private training institutions.
